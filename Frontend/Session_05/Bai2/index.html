Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer-songwriter. Known for her autobiog and the subject of widespread media coverage, with a vast fanbase known as Swifties.

Swift signed to Big Machine Records in 2005, debuting as a country singer with the albums Taylor Swif Me" found crossover success on country and pop radio formats. She incorporated rock on Speak Now set 1989 (2014); the ensuing media scrutiny inspired the hip-hop-imbued Reputation (2017).
